St.Augustine-Tagaste Secondary school.

St. Augustine Tagaste Secondary school is Tanzania located 3 kilometers from Morogoro road which provides both ordinary and advanced secondary level of education to both boys and girls. The school is owned by the Order of Saint Augustine (OSA), which was founded in 1244 in Italy. St. Augustine secondary school is well-founded with all necessities required to encourage learning, from a competent teaching staff, accommodating class rooms, an easily accessible library and every other essential to boost the students’ academic accomplishment. As a school, our primary objective isn’t entirely to obtain academic excellence for the students but also make them be more disciplined

MOTTO

Tolle lege: chukua na usome

MISSION

Providing quality education through best teaching and learning process. Cultivating the spirit of the search for truth as deemed in the patron of this school. Inculcating the sense of transparency, honesty, responsibility, dedication and competence among students

VISION

To enable students acquire skills that help them compete and excel in academics so as to obtain a sound education and become holistic responsible person.
